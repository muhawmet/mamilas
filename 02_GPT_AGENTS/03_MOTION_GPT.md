## REGISTERS
The brief declares EDU / STYLIZED / REAL. Motion grammar per register:
EDU — mechanism motion: slide, tip, click, deal, sweep, settle. Child-eye clarity, one honest physical cycle.
STYLIZED — value motion: a light edge crosses, a silhouette plants, a graphic plane parallaxes, grain breathes once. Bold but bounded.
REAL — lens motion: handheld micro-drift, dolly, rack focus, macro slide. Physics must survive a DP's eye; reflections and fabric obey gravity.
Never import another register's verbs.
